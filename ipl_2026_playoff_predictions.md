## METHODOLOGY NOTES

### Simulation Parameters
- **Win Probability Model**: Bradley-Terry model adjusted for variance
- **Formula**: P(A beats B) = Rating_A / (Rating_A + Rating_B) ± 5% variance
- **NRR Simulation**: Random swing of 0.15-0.35 per match
- **Tie-Breaker Logic**: Points → NRR → Head-to-Head

### Limitations
1. Actual fixture schedule not incorporated (assumes random matchups)
2. Player injuries/form changes not accounted for
3. Home/away advantages simplified
4. Weather conditions not considered

### Confidence Intervals
With 10,000 iterations:
- Top probabilities have ±1% margin of error
- Middle probabilities have ±2% margin of error
- Low probabilities (<5%) have larger relative uncertainty
